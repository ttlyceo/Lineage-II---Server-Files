Borrow Account System allows you borrowyour account to other players withoutaffecting your original password, among other things.
A Barrow Account cannot do: * Trade * Erase Items * Erase Char * Enter to Email System * PK * Drop Items * Change Password/Email * Buy/Sell/Package Sell * Enter to Auction/Bid House
When you re-enter your account withyour master password, the borrow Accountwill be deleted.
